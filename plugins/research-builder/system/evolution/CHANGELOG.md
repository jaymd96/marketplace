# Research Builder Evolution Changelog

## 2026-03-07 — System Created

**What:** Initial research-builder: 5 stance workflows, 4 templates, 3 subagents,
7 skills, 3 scripts. Adapted from spec-builder for research exploration.
**Why:** PhD research is a random walk of ideas that needs tracking across sessions,
linking to prior work, and progressive formalization. Spec-builder's harness
pattern works but the content layer (stances, domain model, output) needed
adapting for research vs product specification.
