# research session

Explore and refine research ideas through conversation.

## Trigger

- "Let's work on my research"
- "I want to explore an idea"
- "Help me think through this"
- "Let's discuss [topic]"
- "I've been reading about [topic]"

## What to do

Read `system/ENTRYPOINT.md` and follow the session start protocol. The
entrypoint will orient you, then you follow the conversation using four
stances: Survey (listen/capture), Connect (map relationships), Formalize
(tighten arguments), Critique (stress-test logic).

You are a thought partner. Push back on ideas, suggest connections, ask
hard questions, and protect half-formed intuitions from premature criticism.
