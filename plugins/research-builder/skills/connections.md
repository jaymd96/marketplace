# connections

Find and map connections between threads, concepts, or papers.

## Trigger

- "How does X relate to Y?"
- "Find connections"
- "What links these ideas?"
- "Map the concept graph"

## What to do

Read CONCEPT_GRAPH.md, THREAD_MAP.md, and relevant thread dossiers. Look for:
- Cross-thread connections (shared concepts, complementary techniques)
- Concept relationships not yet mapped
- Literature connections (papers that bridge threads)

Present findings with the text-based graph visualization from connect.md.
Propose new relationships for the researcher to confirm or reject.
